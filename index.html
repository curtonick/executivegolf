First to Finish 9 Holes Wins

Speedy Golf is a video game project by a solo developer in active development. Compete against the clock or against up to 3 friends locally. Quickly aim and hit golf shots. Don't rush too much, try to stay in the fairway and out of the sand traps.





You also have to run!

In Speedy Golf, it's a footrace to see who can chase down their golf ball first. Avoid the tall grass and water, they'll slow you down. Find shortcuts through the course to post the best times.





Multiplayer Madness

Close finishes down to the second, screwing over your friends, crazy comebacks. Play up to 4 player split-screen. Use a combination of keyboard, mouse, and controller inputs.





Gamemodes Galore

Race the entire course, hole-by-hole, or battle it out with a variety of powerups. Start a Speedy Golf Career and complete objectives to buy memberships at more prestigious and challenging couses.
